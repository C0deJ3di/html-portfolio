The Best Movies According to Andrew Bokowski

My top 3 movies of all-time.

A Beautiful Mind

Mathematician John Nash lives out fantasies through his schizophrenic delusions. Despite his illness, Mr. Nash throughout his teaching and acaedmia career, receives remarkable awards such as the Nobel Piece prize.

Shutter Island

A US Marshal goes to an isolated island to investigate the disappearance of a patient who is a convicted murderer.

Dune

Sci-Fi film set in the future. A noble house (Atreides) is called upon to take over the deadly and inhospitable planet Arrakis.
